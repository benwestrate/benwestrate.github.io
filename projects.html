Iron(Fe)

Iron(Fe)

Iron (Fe) is an opinionated yet flexible FrontEnd development framework.

We want Iron to be a foundation for projects. Like all foundations Iron(Fe) is a place to start, but a foundation is just a beginning. There can be many directions you can go after you have a firm foundation. We want to give front end developers who work with AEM access to the same tools that we have when working outside AEM. Iron makes a few decisions for you.

LumberJack

LumberJack is a modern client side logging framwork. The problems that I was trying to solve with LumberJack are

  1. Clear definition of where each log is comming from.
    • Title of the component where the log comes from
    • Consistant color of the log comming from the component
    • Full stack trace included in each log.
  2. Full server side logging of configured log level
    • including the stack trace.

GGP

Roll :Front End Architect

GGP owns 120 high end retail malls around the US. Each year on Thanksgiving their sites would crash under the load of the Black Friday traffic. The new sites needed to scale up and down with demand of the holidays. They also need to be fully function on mobile devices. We were able to accomplish both of those goals. The sites have not been down once sense we launched in October 2015.
