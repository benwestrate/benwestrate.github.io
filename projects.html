My Move

My Move is a one stop shop for movers. It houses hundreds of articales and blog posts about moving. The site also has diffrent tools to help the mover estimate the cost of thier move. Some of these include a paint calculator and a moving cost calculator.

The real meat of the project was building out a searies of single page applications for thier exlusive deals. These apps allow the customer to select one or many deals and have them sent to their email.

LumberJack

  1. Clear definition of where each log is comming from.
    • Title of the component where the log comes from
    • Consistant color of the log comming from the component
    • Full stack trace included in each log.
  2. Full server side logging of configured log level
    • including the stack trace.
